## Usage

1. Click on the _project_ icon (<FontAwesomeIcon icon={faKeadexMina} className="inline w-4 mx-2" />) in the sidebar
2. Click on the _settings_ button
3. Click on the _themes_ tab
4. Customize the colors with your preferred ones
5. Click on the _save_ button
6. Enjoy your customized diagrams theme
